About SmartSchool

The multipurpose system

We provide high quality and cost effective school management system for schools which helps in easy, effortless and safer administration. The system allows for customization that suit your needs.

  • Other Products

SmartSchool Features

Every module of our system is customizable to the needs of your school. The system is highly flexible and can be modified.

Administration

Admission, class and subjects management. Dashboards with miniature reports, lunch management

Academics

Subjects, course work, exercises, assignments, resources, examination, terminal report

Portal/Mobile App

Access bills, payments, terminal reports, announcements, notifications, receive notification, bulk sms


Library

Available books, borrowed books, returned books, due books, over due books, book quantity

Analytics

Simple dashboards and analytics for informed decision making. Access to on the go reports

Finance

Students billing, payments, students and suppliers ledgers, expenses, banking, etc.
